Fitness & Movement
Mobility Cheat Sheet
12 stretches · Hips · Shoulders · Hamstrings · Hold 30–60 sec

Hips

  • 90/90 Hip Stretch — Both legs at 90°, hinge forward over the front shin. 60 sec/side · Hip rotators
  • Pigeon Pose — Knee forward toward same-side wrist, hips low, back leg long. Fold forward to deepen. 60 sec/side · Piriformis, glutes
  • Low Lunge (Crescent) — Back knee down, drive hips forward. Arms overhead to intensify. 45 sec/side · Hip flexors, psoas
  • Butterfly Stretch — Soles together, press knees toward the floor using elbows. 45 sec · Inner hip, groin

Shoulders

  • Cross-Body Shoulder Pull — Arm across chest, opposite forearm presses it closer. Shoulder blades down. 30 sec/side · Posterior deltoid
  • Doorway Pec Stretch — Forearms on frame at 90°, lean chest through. 30–45 sec · Pecs, anterior delt
  • Sleeper Stretch — Lie on side, bottom arm at 90°, top hand presses wrist toward floor. 30 sec/side · Posterior capsule
  • Thread the Needle — All fours, slide one arm under until shoulder and cheek rest on the floor. 45 sec/side · Thoracic, rotator cuff

Hamstrings

  • Standing Forward Fold — Hinge at hips, micro-bend knees, grab opposite elbows and sway. 45–60 sec · Full hamstring chain
  • Supine Single-Leg Pull — Strap around one foot, extend toward ceiling, other leg flat. 45 sec/side · Hamstrings, calf
  • Seated Forward Fold — Legs straight, hinge from hips (not lower back), spine long. 60 sec · Biceps femoris
  • Half-Split (Lizard Variant) — From a lunge, shift back and straighten the front leg. Hips square, back flat. 45 sec/side · Proximal hamstring

Tips: Never bounce — hold steady and breathe into the stretch. Morning is ideal for gentle mobility work; post-workout is best for deeper holds. Consistency beats intensity: 10 min daily beats 1 hr weekly.
